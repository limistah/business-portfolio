Omerian Foundation

Omerian Foundation is the social enterprise arm of our organization. We implement initiatives specially tailored for the youths focusing on TEE: Technology, Education, and Entrepreneurship.

Technology

We endeavour to instill into young people the love of technology and the spirit of continuous innovation.

Entrepreneurship

We seek to impart into youths the skills needed to be their own bosses. We encourage them to build businesses around their talents and skills, of which each person has at least one.

Enducation

We explore avenues of remedying the poor quality of education which is currently obtainable in Nigeria. We encourage them to love books because, according to Lekan Fashina, “the habit of reading is a goldmine.”
